Snake Finder Privacy Policy
Snake Finder is an app that compares follower and following lists. This Snake Finder Privacy Policy describes how Snake Finder interacts with Instagram and uses that information.


Information Access
We access your information from Instagram APIs for Snake Finder and we may store that information to improve Snake Finder. Your information is not disclosed to any third party. However, we may use aggregate data or other anonymous infomation. This Privacy Policy may be revised.
